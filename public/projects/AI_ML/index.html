Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning Projects

   Google DeepDream - Here is my attempt at jumping headfirst into the world of computer vision research. I'm beginning my trek with Google DeepDream. DeepDream is an approach created by Google engineer Alexander Mordvintsev that causes convolutional neural nets to divulge their secrets. Whereas traditional CNNs take images as input and produce a classification, DeepDreaming CNNs work backward. This means that they modify pictures to look more like the things they classify. Running the DeepDream algorithm in multiple iterations causes the CNN to over-analyze the image to the point where it is unrecognizable and covered by trippy fractals reminiscent of the training dataset.
